Television Aerials:

We typically supply & install: UHF wideband aerials for "Freeview" terrestrial reception.

Average cost: Wide band digital compatible UHF aerial, up-to 6ft heavy gauge alloy mast, 6-9" galvanized wall or chimney fittings, up-to 25 meters coaxial cable (colour: brown, white or black), approximately 1.5 hours labour, coaxial plug, set-up & re-tune/demonstration.

Total estimated cost: £222.00

Loft mounted installations estimated cost: £188.00

The final cost may be higher or lower depending upon the signals received. If the required signal levels are not received then additional materials/labour may be required i.e. taller mast or signal amplification. Please note that if there is necessity to acquire additional signal levels then I would seek correct approval/permission prior to any un-foreseen expense.

Please note: Television receiving aerials provide optimum reception via a clear signal path from their mounted position to designated transmitter, if the line of sight to transmitter is obstructed by trees/buildings etc, additional mast length/signal amplification may be required.

Radio Aerials FM/DB:

Omni directional (receives signals via 360 degrees) or multi elements for directional reception and to acquire increased signal levels We commonly install omni directional radio aerials as they are more discrete in size and produce the appropriate signal reception within the local area (Horsham).

All aerials are supplied with high quality coaxial cable to ensure high signal transfer and optimum interference rejection. Loft fittings, wall or chimney, via heavy gauge alloy mast & single/double/triple support fittings (non flimsy materials so to shorten the life span of the installation)
